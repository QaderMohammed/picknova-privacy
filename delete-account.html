Delete Your PickNova Account

If you would like to permanently delete your PickNova account and associated personal data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the PickNova app.
  2. Go to Profile Settings Delete Account.
  3. Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

After confirmation, your account and associated personal information will be permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.
